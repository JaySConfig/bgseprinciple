Understanding Your Energy System

Not everyone is wired the same way. Understanding your energy system — and others' — can prevent conflict and help you make better life choices.

High-Energy Systems

Some people are naturally wired for high output and stimulation. High-energy people typically:

  • Need novelty, challenge, and growth to feel fulfilled
  • Get restless and depressed when understimulated
  • Find routine and predictability draining
  • Require mental and physical engagement to feel balanced
  • See change as exciting
  • Feel energised by taking on new challenges

When understimulated, they experience:

  • Physical restlessness and tension
  • Mental fog and depression
  • Feeling like they’re "wasting away"
  • A desperate need to escape familiar environments

Low-Energy Systems

Other people are naturally wired for stability and conservation. Low-energy people typically:

  • Find comfort in routine and predictability
  • Feel overwhelmed by too much novelty or change
  • Prefer familiar environments and known quantities
  • See change as potentially threatening
  • Need stability to function well
  • Feel energised by maintaining rather than pursuing

When overstimulated, they experience:

  • Anxiety and overwhelm
  • Desire to retreat to familiar comfort zones
  • Feeling like change is too much
  • Need for stability and routine to rebalance

The Mismatch Problem

Conflicts arise when people with different energy systems try to impose their needs on each other:

High-energy person to low-energy: "You’re too comfortable — you need to grow and take risks!"
Low-energy person to high-energy: "You’re too restless — you need to settle down and be content!"

Neither is wrong — they just have different operating systems.

Know Your System

Understanding your natural energy system helps you:

  • Make life choices that work with your nature, not against it
  • Stop judging yourself by others’ standards
  • Choose environments and relationships that support rather than drain you
  • Recognise when conflicts are about different wiring, not character flaws

The Key Insight

Your energy system isn’t a choice — it’s how you’re built. The choice is whether you honour it or fight against it. Work with your wiring, not against it.
